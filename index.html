A veteran, web developer, healthcare manager, entrepreneur, musician and fisherman, I began studying software development from the confines of an Air Force tent while deployed to Helmand Province, Afghanistan in 2012.

In April 2015, I completed an intensive 12 week Javascript course at the Iron Yard in Austin, TX where my skills were honed on the afore mentioned languages as well as on two Javascript frameworks: Angular and Backbone.
