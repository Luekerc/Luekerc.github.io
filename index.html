My History With Software

My first forray into software development began when I was 9 years old. I started learning BASIC on a Radio Shack TRS-80 . The pinnacle of my early efforts was changing the screen color then repeating the same word down the left side.

While deployed to Helmand Province, Afghanistan in 2012, I casually started reading up on more modern languages like HTML and CSS. I've since used platforms such as Wordpress and Shopify for blogging and online sales. In April 2015, I completed an intensive 12 week JavaScript course at The Iron Yard in Austin, TX where my skills were honed on JavaScript as well as on associated frameworks: Node, Angular, Backbone and Sails.
